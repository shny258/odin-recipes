Fried Chicken Sandwich

This is a common dish from my country, Argentina. But also common in western countries.

The basics of this dish is fried chicken between french bread cut in two. After that you can add whatever you want, from tomatoes slices, garlic, lettuce to all the sausage you want!.

We are gonna do a classic one.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pan
  • 100 ml Oil
  • 1 French bread
  • 1 Fried chicken
  • 2 Lettuce leafs
  • 1 Tomato
  • Garlic Rings
  • Mayonnaise, ketchup or your favorite sausage

Steps

  1. Pour oil in the pan
  2. Heat up the oil on the pan in your kitchen
  3. Fry the chicken
  4. Cut the bread
  5. Pour the sausage
  6. Add tomato slices, garlic and lettuce
  7. Put the chicken
  8. Enjoy!
