Pesto Pasta with Chicken

Description

his chicken pesto pasta is easy and delicious. Serve with crusty bread and salad for a quick dinner. Use as much or as little pesto sauce as you like. Using homemade pesto will taste even better, but it adds to prep time. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1(16 ounce) package bow tie pasta
  • 1 teaspoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup pesto sauce
  • 1/3 cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained and cut into strips

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saut√© garlic until tender, then stir in chicken and season with red pepper flakes. Cook until chicken is golden and cooked through.
  3. Combine pasta, chicken, pesto, and sun-dried tomatoes in a large bowl; toss to coat evenly.
