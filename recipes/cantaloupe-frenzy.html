Cantaloupe Frenzy

A cantaloupe smoothie!! Perfect for those hot summer days or an after school snack to cool down with!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cantaloupe - peeled, seeded and cubed
  • 3 Tablespoon white sugar, or to taste
  • 2 Cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place cantaloupes cubes and ice into the container of a blender
  2. Process until the ice is in small pieces
  3. Add sugar, and puree
  4. Pour into tall glasses and serve immediately
  5. Enjoy!
